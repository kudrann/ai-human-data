Furthermore, the use of technology has also had a negative impact on our mental health. Studies have shown that excessive screen time can lead to addiction, insomnia, and other sleep disorders. The blue light emitted by screens can also disrupt our natural circadian rhythms, making it difficult for us to fall asleep at night.

In conclusion, while computers have undoubtedly revolutionized the way we live and work, their negative effects on our physical and mental health cannot be ignored.
