 Dear Editor,

As someone who has grown up in a world where technology is constantly advancing, I firmly believe that computers have had an overall positive effect on society. While it is true that some people may spend too much time on their screens and neglect other aspects of their lives, the benefits of computers far outweigh any potential drawbacks.

One major advantage of computers is the ability to learn about different cultures and places around the world. With just a few clicks, we can access information about any topic imaginable, allowing us to expand our knowledge and understanding of others. This global connection that technology provides has helped foster empathy and compassion among people from all walks of life.

In addition, computers have revolutionized the way we work and communicate with one another. Gone are the days when we had to send letters or make phone calls to connect with someone on the other side of the world. With video conferencing software and instant messaging apps, we can now communicate in real-time with anyone, anywhere at any time. This has greatly improved productivity in the workplace and made it easier for people to stay connected with their loved ones no matter where they are.

Another benefit of computers is that they teach hand-eye coordination, which can be useful in various aspects of life. From playing video games to using graphic design software, computers offer many opportunities for individuals to improve their fine motor skills and hand-eye coordination.

Of course, it's essential to strike a balance between our online lives and the physical world around us. It would be best if we made an effort not to neglect the importance of exercise, spending time outdoors, and nurturing our relationships with friends and family. However, when used responsibly, computers can be incredibly powerful tools that benefit society as a whole.

In conclusion, I believe that computers have had a positive effect on people and society at large. By providing us with opportunities to learn about the world around us, improving communication and productivity, and offering countless other benefits, computers continue to shape our lives for the better.
