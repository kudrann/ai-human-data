One area where computers have had a particularly positive impact is in the field of medicine. With the help of computer technology, doctors and medical researchers can now diagnose diseases more accurately, develop new treatments, and improve patient care.

Some people worry that we're spending too much time in front of screens and not enough time enjoying the natural world or connecting with others face-to-face.
