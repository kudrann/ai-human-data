 Dear Editor,
I am writing this letter to express my opinion on the impact of computers on society. As someone who has grown up with technology, I firmly believe that computers have had a positive effect on people.

This exposure to different perspectives broadens our understanding of the world and helps us become more empathetic and globally-minded individuals.
Furthermore, computers have revolutionized communication. With social media and messaging platforms, people can connect with friends and family from all over the world instantly. This has made our global community feel smaller and more connected than ever before.
