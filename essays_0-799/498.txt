I am writing to express my viewpoint regarding the impact of computers on society. While it is true that computers have revolutionized the way we live and work, I believe that they also present some challenges that need to be addressed.

However, there are some negative aspects to consider as well. In conclusion, I believe that computers have brought many benefits to society, but we must also be mindful of the potential drawbacks.
