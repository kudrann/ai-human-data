 Dear Editor,

I am writing this letter to express my thoughts on the impact of computers on society. I believe that while technology has brought about significant advancements and conveniences, it has also had some negative consequences.

Additionally, computer-related jobs are in high demand, providing people with opportunities for employment and income.

However, there is a growing concern that computers are causing us to become more isolated and detached from real-life experiences. Furthermore, excessive computer use can lead to social isolation, as people become more engrossed in their online activities and neglect the importance of face-to-face interactions with friends and family.

In conclusion, while computers have undeniably revolutionized our world, it is essential that we remain mindful of the potential negative consequences they may bring. It is crucial to find a balance between embracing technology and maintaining a healthy lifestyle full of real-life experiences and connections.
