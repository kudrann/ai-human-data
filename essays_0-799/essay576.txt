 Dear Editor,

I am writing this letter to express my thoughts on the impact of computers on society. As a technology enthusiast, I firmly believe that computers have greatly benefited humanity in various ways.

People are spending more time sitting behind screens, which can lead to a sedentary lifestyle and various health issues. This is why it's crucial for us to maintain a balance between our online and offline lives.

In conclusion, I believe that computers have brought numerous benefits to society, but we must also be mindful of the potential drawbacks.
